The Problem

Sport horse management is scattered across a dozen tools.

Paper passports, FEI documents, vet records, training journals, competition sites, and endless WhatsApp threads with trainers and vets. Important details get lost between them — and a missed vaccination or forgotten withdrawal period can cost an entire season.

Paper horse passports FEI documents Veterinary records Training journals Competition websites Nutrition notes WhatsApp with trainers & vets Spreadsheets Calendar reminders

One missed detail can end a season. StableSphere exists so it never does.

The Vision

A single, secure ecosystem for the modern competitive rider.

StableSphere replaces the fragmented combination of paper, spreadsheets and messaging apps with one customizable platform — from everyday horse care to international FEI competitions.

Health & Care

Every record, in one place.

Vaccinations, diagnoses, medication and rehabilitation — a complete, secure medical history that's never lost again.

Performance

Every ride, tracked.

Training logs, competition results and long-term performance graphs to visualize real development.

Competition & Feeding

Every detail, prepared.

International travel requirements, packing checklists and personalized nutrition plans — ready before you need them.

Signature Features

Built for FEI-level detail.

Eight tools that turn scattered paperwork into one calm, reliable system — built specifically for competitive horse owners.

Competition Readiness Score

A single number that tells you whether your horse is cleared, cautioned, or blocked from competing.

FEI Passport & Documentation

Microchip, passport, insurance and ownership records — always current, always accessible.

Vaccination Timeline

A chronological record of every vaccination, with reminders before validity expires.

Medication & Withdrawal Tracker

Log every treatment and see the estimated withdrawal countdown before competition.

AI Toxic Plant Scanner

Scan pasture plants with your camera and instantly see safe, caution, or toxic ratings.

Nutrition & Feeding Planner

Personalized feed plans, supplements and reminders for every horse, stored and refined over time.

International Competition Assistant

Country requirements, transport rules, packing checklists and nearby emergency vets — before you travel.

Curated Marketplace

Buy and sell saddles, equipment and horses in a marketplace built for quality, not clutter.
